Endangered Language Conservation Project -


This project isn't finished, but it's one that I founded myself. The vision is to create a language-learning app one day that focuses on bringing endangered languages back and preserving them for posterity. I had the idea because my wife is a member of the Ponca Tribe of Nebraska, one of the smallest indigenous tribes in the US. Fewer than 50 people speak the tribe's language fluently. I wanted my wife to have the chance to learn it, so I tracked down a difficult to find dictionary. She was so happy when I gave it to her that I just wanted to bring that joy to other people.
